## Course Description

Covers principles of Design for Manufacturing and Assembly (DFMA). Topics include:

- What is DFMA and its benefits
- Material and process selection
- Part count reduction
- Design for: manual assembly, electrical assembly, robotic assembly, machining, sheet metal working, hot forging
- Tolerancing and statistical analysis of tolerancing
- Design for Disassembly

**Prerequisite:** Undergraduate mathematics and probability theory
