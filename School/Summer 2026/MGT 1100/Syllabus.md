## Course Description

Introduces students to the global business environment. Covers how differences in economic systems, national culture, socio-demographics, and political orientations affect business operations. Also introduces key business activities.

Satisfies OU general education requirements for **global perspective** and **writing intensive**.

**Prerequisite:** Completion of OU writing foundation requirement (WRT/RHT160 with at least a 2.0). Recommended for non-SBA majors or business students without major standing.
